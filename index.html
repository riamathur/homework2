Info about NJIT

New Jersey Institute of Technology is a public institution that was founded in 1881. It has a total undergraduate enrollment of 8,211, its setting is urban, and the campus size is 45 acres. It utilizes a semester-based academic calendar. New Jersey Institute of Technology's ranking in the 2018 edition of Best Colleges is National Universities, 140. Its in-state tuition and fees are $16,430 (2017-18); out-of-state tuition and fees are $31,034 (2017-18).
